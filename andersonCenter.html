Immanuel Hall

302 South Grant Street

Built in 1900 by German craftsmen, this former church stands as a symbol of Hinsdaleâ€™s spirit and sense of community. It has served not only as a church, but also as office space and a preschool. The building was saved from demolition through the efforts of the Hinsdale Historical Society, government officials and residents. Now on the National Register of Historic Places, Immanuel Hall continues to serve the community as a place available for public use.
